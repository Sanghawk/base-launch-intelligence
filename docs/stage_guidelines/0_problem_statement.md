# Stage 0 — Strategy Framing

**Goal:** Decide what business/product you are actually building.

**What needs to be accomplished:**

- Define the core product thesis.
- Decide whether this is for personal use, paid users, analysts, traders, or builders.
- Clarify that the product is focused on **launch quality, risk, liquidity survivability, and attention intelligence**.
- Define what the product will explicitly not do: execution bots, copytrading, public-mempool sniping, generic wallet labels, or black-box price prediction.
- Reduce the idea to a single clear product sentence.

**Gate:** Can you explain the product in one sentence?
