# Stage 7 — Validation Beta

**Goal:** Test against users and public baselines.

**What needs to be accomplished:**

- Compare your rankings against public baselines like DEX Screener new pairs, volume rankings, boosts, and GeckoTerminal pool data.
- Measure whether your top-ranked launches have better liquidity retention, lower scam incidence, or cleaner attention signals.
- Let a small number of users test the workflow.
- Track whether users trust the score explanations.
- Measure alert usefulness and alert fatigue.
- Validate whether users would pay for this as a monitoring and triage tool.

**Gate:** Does your ranking beat simple public dashboards enough to matter?
